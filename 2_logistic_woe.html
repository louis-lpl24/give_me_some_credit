Define final transformations¶

Map bins to their WoE values

  1. WoETransform - will drop highly-correlated variables as before. However, manual checking reveals that NumberOfTimes90DaysLate is a lot more bunched up than NumberOfTime30-59DaysPastDueNotWorse (>94% are 0-value vs ~84% for the latter) - so we will drop NumberOfTimes90DaysLate instead
  2. WoETransformV2 - won't drop any of the correlated past due and late count variables as Attempt 1 shows that it is boosts performance
